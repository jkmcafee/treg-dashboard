How TurboRegime Works

You don’t have to constantly watch the market. TurboRegime monitors market conditions, detects regime changes, and shows how Jeff is positioned.

What TReg Does

TurboRegime is a rules-based market regime system designed for my own use to participate in upside opportunities while attempting to reduce exposure during periods of elevated risk. Over time, I decided to make the system available to others who may find it useful.
